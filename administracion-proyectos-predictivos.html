Training

Predictive Project Management Course

Predictive | Agile | Hybrid

Objetive

Learn the concepts, techniques and tools that help to standardize the language in the management of predictive projects and its application to real situations to immediately replicate the knowledge to your projects, having as a reference framework the best management practices established by the PMI® , with different levels of depth.

Format

This course is available in face-to-face and online mode, in which, through selected tools, active interaction of participants is promoted in real time.

There are two versions, depending on the number of practices, level of depth and exercises that are carried out:

Face-to-face and Online

  • 24 Hours (Basic)

  • 40 Hours (Intermediate)

Who is it aimed at

JHeads of area, leaders, administrators and project team members responsible for the definition, planning and / or execution of your organization's projects.

Dynamic

  • Theoretical-practical approach where group participation is encouraged.
  • The facilitators present a brief explanation of each concept, solved cases are discussed and dynamics are carried out to reinforce the knowledge presented.
  • Participants apply techniques and tools of predictive Project Management learned in teams generating documentation and planning of a project.

Benefits

on how to apply the acquired knowledge to their predictive projects.
