Solution

We have specialized and committed consultants, experts in various topics to solve the specific needs of your organization. We specialize in Project Management, Microsoft Technology for Project Management and Change Management.

Solución

Contamos con consultores especializados y comprometidos, expertos en diversos temas para solucionar las necesidades específicas de su organización. Nos especializamos en Gestión de Proyectos, Tecnología Microsoft para Gestión de Proyectos y Administración del Cambio.

Our consultants have comprehensive training, knowledge and experience in different specialties:

I. Project Management

We monitor the life cycle of initiatives, portfolio, programs and projects, in different areas and management practices:

  • Predictive
  • Adaptive (agile or hybrid)

II. Microsoft Technology for Project Management

Support our clients with specialists in Microsoft Technology implementation processes:

  • Project Online and Enterprise Project Management (EPM)
  • Teams
  • Power BI

III. Change management

We support our clients to adapt to change with the pillars of our People & Change methodology: communication, training, sponsorship and awareness, based on the international Prosci standard.
